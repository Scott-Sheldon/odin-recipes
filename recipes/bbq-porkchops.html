BBQ Pork Chops

Sticky, Sweet and Smokey BBQ Pork Chops

A great Summertime meal that goes well with rice and vegtables.

Ingredients

  • 4 1 inch thick Pork Chops
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Dried Rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp Mustard Powder

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients besides the Pork Chops together in a small bowl.
  2. Pat dry your Pork Chops and then cover them liberaly with the spice mix.
  3. Grill on medium high heat until the Pork reaches a internal tempurture of 165 degres farenheit.
  4. Let the Pork rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve with Rice and vegtables.

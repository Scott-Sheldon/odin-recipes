Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Delicous Noodles with Ground Meat and Tomato Sauce

A classic family meal that the kids can help make!

It only takes a few ingredients and no time at all to cook.

Ingredients

  • A box of Spaghetti Noodles
  • A pound of Ground Meat
  • A jar of Tomato Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Filled large stock pot with salted water and place on the stove to boil.
  2. In another pot on medium high heat and add a small amount of olive oil ground meat.
  3. While the meat is browning make sure to break up the pieces with a wooden spoon.
  4. Once the meat is browned you can choose to drain the excess fat or leave it in.
  5. Add your jar of Tomato Sauce to the ground meat and reduce heat to medium low.
  6. When your water begins to boil add your noodles and cook according to the packages directions.
  7. When pasta is finished drain and toss with a little Olive oil.
  8. Plate the Spaghetti and top with the Meat Sauce.

Grilled Cheese

Melty Cheese inbetween toasted bread

Perfect with Tomato Soup this classic sandwich is so easy anyone can make it!

It only takes a few ingredients and no time at all to cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both slices of bread on one side each.
  2. Place sliced cheese on top of buttered side of bread.
  3. Place the other slice of bread on top with the buttered side on the outside of the sandwich.
  4. Place the sandwich into a frying pan on medium heat with the butter toast facing down.
  5. Butter the top of the sandwich.
  6. Once and first side has reached it's desired toastiness then flip the sandwich over and cook till finished.
